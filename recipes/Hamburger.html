Hamburger

The juiciest hamburger ever

This hamburger is the best one you will ever try due to its succulent ingredients and it is easy as pie to make, why do not give it a try?

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons evaporated milk
  • 1⁄8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat a grill for a high heat
  3. Mix ground beef,egg, bread crumbs, and garlic
  4. Make 8 patties
  5. Lightly oil the grill grate. Grill patties until browned and no longer pink, about 5 minutes per side.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy
