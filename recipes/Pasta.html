Pasta recipe

A great pasta recipe for those summer moments with family and friends. The pasta will make you travel back to a small italian town.

Ingredients

  • 1 package tri-color pasta
  • 3⁄4 pound Italian salami
  • 1⁄2Red onion, chopped
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella balls
  • 1 cup italian-style salad dressing
  • 1 can sliced black olives
  • 1⁄2cup Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Cook rotini pasta at a boil until tender yest firm to the bite, about 8 minute. Drain and rinse with cold water until cool.
  3. Combine salami, onion, mozarella balls, and olives
  4. Season pasta salad with dry salad dressing mix and stir to combine.
  5. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese before serving. Enjoy!
